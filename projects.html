About Me

My name is Jack MarionSims, and I'm a Junior at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I am studying Artificial Intelligence and Decision Making along with Brain and Cognitive Science, and I plan to graduate in 2026. I am most interested in the parallels between a human brain and artificial intelligence and how we can use one to learn or infer about the other. Technically, I am most familiar with Python, but I also have experience using various other languages such as Java or Swift. Recently, I have gotten more interested in game development, so I have been using the Unity game engine which also uses C#, and I am currently working on creating a 2 player platformer in order to grow accustomed to Unity and C#. If you have any questions about me or what I'm working on, or if you want to know more, feel free to reach out through the Contact Me section at the top of the page.
